Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our OTT platform.

  • How do I create an account?
  • How can I start watching content?
  • What devices are supported?
  • How do I reset my password?

How do I create an account?

To create an account, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website.
  2. Click the "Sign Up" button.
  3. Fill out the registration form with your details.
  4. Click "Create Account."

How can I start watching content?

To start watching content:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Browse or search for the content you want to watch.
  3. Click on the content thumbnail.
  4. Click the "Play" button.

What devices are supported?

Our platform is compatible with various devices, including:

  • Smartphones
  • Tablets
  • Smart TVs
  • Streaming devices (e.g., Roku, Amazon Fire TV)
  • Web browsers (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, etc.)

How do I reset my password?

If you forget your password:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Click "Forgot Password."
  3. Enter your email address.
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
